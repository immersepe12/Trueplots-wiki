## Why people look for Swasya Living alternatives

Four themes come up consistently among buyers evaluating Swasya:

- **Sakleshpur is 4 hours from Bangalore.** Sannidhi sits at ~220km via NH-75 from Bangalore. For a weekend retreat, most urban buyers prefer 60–120 minutes. Kanakapura, Mandya, Hosur, and Doddaballapur offer comparable landscape at 1.5× closer distance.
- **Premium pricing is priced-in.** At ~₹309/sqft, Sannidhi is 5–8× raw plantation land in the same Hassan belt (₹15–25L/acre typical, ₹42–47L/acre for premium coffee estates). That premium pays for amenities, not land appreciation.
- **Location lock-in.** Swasya sells only in Sakleshpur and Kanakapura (Vana Vida). If your priority is a specific taluk, water source, or road connectivity, you're out of options.
- **Construction and exit terms undefined.** Swasya's site doesn't publish maintenance fees or resale mechanics. This is standard in the managed-farmland category — TruePlots' transparency wedge.
