## The single most important lesson from Spinny

**Spinny ties fixed pricing to visible verification — with numerical scores, not pass/fail dots.** On every listing, Spinny's quality report shows "1573 parts evaluated by 5 automotive experts" + 5 category scores each /10 (Core 9.6, Supporting 9.2, etc.) + trust chips ("Meter not tampered / Non-flooded / Core structure intact"). Price and scored verification reinforce each other.

This is the single pattern TruePlots should copy most precisely. Not just "embedded report" — **numerical scores per category, plus explicit trust chips attacking known fraud patterns (PTCL / encumbrance / 79A-79B / boundary)**. The buyer shifts from "is this plot fairly priced?" anxiety to "I can see exactly what passed and at what score."

---

## What Spinny is and isn't

**Spinny's differentiated assets:** 200-point inspection (category framing, not full list), fixed non-negotiable pricing, 5-day money-back, 1-year warranty, Sachin Tendulkar celebrity anchor, Spinny Capital unified finance brand, moderate-not-aggressive financing push, emotional brand voice ("Guaranteed Love" / "Khushiyon ki Long Drive").

**Spinny's scale:** ₹3,725 Cr FY24 revenue (+14%), ₹590 Cr net loss (–28%), 57 hubs across 22 cities, 200,000 cars delivered cumulatively, ~$500M total raised, 2025 pre-IPO Series F at flat ~$1.7B valuation. Not yet profitable; IPO-track.

**What Spinny is NOT:** a facilitator model. Spinny buys and holds inventory. TruePlots must explicitly reject this pattern and operate capital-light.

## Top 15 build priorities (ranked) — updated post-screenshot

### Tier 1 — Launch essentials (months 1–3)

**1. Three-layer verification framing** ⭐ NEW (rank-1)
- **Badge:** "120-point verification" (repeated everywhere)
- **LDP detail:** "48 documents reviewed · 12 database cross-checks · 4 field verifications · 3 legal experts" (specific, human-anchored)
- **Score-visible:** 10 categories each scored /10 + trust chips ("PTCL clear · Non-encumbered · 79A/79B compliant · Boundary matches KGIS · Title chain intact")
This is the single most important pattern. Spinny moved from "200-points" to "1573 parts / 5 experts" on their LDP — same granularity must apply to TruePlots.

**2. Fixed-pricing assurance next to scored verification**
Price and verification score shown together. No buyer-side negotiation. If seller insists on unjustified pricing, decline to list.

**3. Four-pillar promises, named consistently** ⭐ UPDATED
120-Point Verification · 12-Month Title Protection · 7-Day Refund · Fixed Price Assurance. Four cards (matches Spinny's exact pattern in screenshot 03), photo-driven, one-line each, repeated verbatim on homepage, listings page, AND LDP.

**4. Photo-first hero with Buy/Sell duality** ⭐ NEW
Single-word headline over dominant photograph. Two hero variants (buy + sell). Single primary CTA. Mirrors Spinny "the master" pattern in screenshots 01-02.

**5. Trust copy embedded IN CTA buttons** ⭐ NEW
"BOOK SITE VISIT · 100% refundable" on purple button. "RESERVE PLOT ₹5,000 refundable" on red button. Trust claim is ON the button itself, not next to it. Major pattern in screenshot 21.

**6. LDP uses tab navigation, not long-scroll** ⭐ NEW (previously assumed scroll)
Five tabs: Overview / Verification Report / TruePlots Protection / Specifications & Features / Financing. Sticky right-side price block. Matches Spinny's pattern in screenshots 21-26.

**7. ₹5,000 refundable reserve for 3-day plot hold** ⭐ NEW (previously ₹50K)
Low-friction hold amount. Honest 3-day countdown. Matches Spinny's reserve pattern in screenshot 30.

**8. 7-day title-defect refund window**
Narrow scope: refund TruePlots coordination + verification fees if material title defect discovered within 7 days post-registration. NOT buyer's-remorse window.

**9. Soft pre-approval financing flow**
"Check your loan eligibility in 3 minutes — no credit score impact." Single partner bank (HDFC or Axis) for launch. Personalized EMI on listing cards post pre-approval.

### Tier 2 — v1.5 (months 4-6)

**10. Per-listing hand-written micro-tag** ⭐ NEW
Every listing card has a 3-5 word editorial-ops-authored distinguishing line. "Corner plot · Mango grove" / "Low PTCL risk · Clean chain" / "5 min from metro". Hire 2-3 editorial-ops alongside verification ops.

**11. 12-month standard title indemnity, bundled**
Partner with HDFC Ergo / ICICI Lombard / Tata AIG. Cover defects up to 50% property value. Internal cost ~₹5-10K per deal, absorbed by TruePlots.

**12. Fractional coordination flow with individual title subdivision**
No Spinny parallel — first principles. Target 20+ completed subdivisions by month 9 as defensibility flip.

**13. Drone/ground video walkthroughs + WhatsApp share row**
Drone for farmland, ground for urban. Plus WhatsApp/social share row on LDP price block (supports referral — Spinny reports 35% buyers via referral in screenshot 12).

### Tier 3 — v2 (months 6-12)

**14. 60-month premium title indemnity (paid SKU)**
Mirror Spinny Assured+. ₹25-75K per buyer. Target 15-25% attach rate.

**15. B-Khata → A-Khata conversion service + 4 adjacent service cards on homepage** ⭐ NEW
- B-Khata → A-Khata: supply-side unlock, ₹50K-1.5L per conversion
- Homepage service grid: Verify / Register / Convert / Construct (mirrors Spinny's Loan/Buyback/FASTag/Challan retention pattern in screenshot 10)

## What specifically NOT to copy

See [09-what-does-not-translate.md](09-what-does-not-translate.md) for the full list. Summary:

1. Buy-and-hold inventory model (breaks facilitator thesis)
2. Fast-purchase decision cycle (7-14 days impossible for plots)
3. Volume-driven TV advertising (wasteful at early stage)
4. Spinny Buyback / guaranteed resale (requires capital model TruePlots doesn't have)
5. Aggressive financing push (damages trust positioning)
6. Celebrity endorsement pre-traction
7. 57-hub physical footprint (plot buyers don't visit TruePlots offices)
8. "300-point inspection" escalation (numbers should reflect reality)
9. 360° immersive view (drone/video is the right paradigm for plots)
10. Loyalty programs / repeat-customer CRM (plot buyers transact once per decade)
11. Price-as-hook marketing (attracts wrong buyer)

---

## Three decisions this analysis should drive

**1. Adopt Spinny's pricing-meets-verification UX pattern as the hero interaction on every listing.** This is the highest-leverage pattern in the research. Ship it on day 1.

**2. Build the trust architecture in the right sequence.** Verification + fixed pricing + refund window + badge tier = launch bundle (month 1-3). Title indemnity + fractional flow = v1.5 (month 4-6). Extended indemnity + conversion services = v2 (month 6-12). Do NOT try to ship everything at once; Spinny itself took 5+ years to layer these.

**3. Make the service layer the moat, not the marketplace UI.** Marketplace listings can be copied by NoBroker in one quarter. The 120-point verification SOP + 3-buyer subdivision operational playbook + BBMP/revenue-department relationships + escrow architecture is what's defensible. Invest engineering-design time in the service stack, not the landing page.
