## C. CRITICAL — Fractional urban plots whitespace

**FINDING: Empty whitespace for legal subdivision-to-individual-title fractional urban plots.**

- India's entire "fractional real estate" category — hBits, Strata, PropShare, Assetmonk, Brikitt, Claravest, PropFTX, SM REITs — is overwhelmingly **commercial Grade A** (offices, warehouses). Driven by SEBI SM REIT framework 2024. All are SPV/unit-share products; **no investor receives individual plot title**. ([KPMG 2024 fractional ownership report](https://assets.kpmg.com/content/dam/kpmgsites/in/pdf/2024/07/the-new-frontier-in-real-estate-investment-unlocking-the-potential-through-fractional-ownership.pdf.coredownload.inline.pdf))
- Khata bifurcation (plot subdivision) IS available as a one-off professional service from **Khata Broker, EzyLegal, Bino.bot** — but these are post-hoc paper-filers, NOT buyer-matchmakers or marketplaces. Fees ~₹5K–15K per bifurcation; 3–6 month processing. ([Khata Broker](https://khatabroker.in/blog/khata-bifurcation/), [Bino](https://bino.bot/find/khata-bifurcation-amalgamation-bengaluru))
- Latent demand is visible: Kaanoon threads like *"Registering 60×40 as two 30×40 plots in Bangalore,"* *"Can 2 cousins split 60×40 BIAPPA plot,"* and Quora threads on joint plot purchase by friends — all asking for a solution that doesn't exist productised. ([Kaanoon 60×40 split](https://www.kaanoon.com/383732/registering-60-40-as-2-30-40-plots-in-bangalore), [Kaanoon cousins split](https://www.kaanoon.com/290478/can-2-cousins-split-60-40-biappa-released-plot-in-bangalore))
- r/bangalore and r/realestateIndia surface joint-purchase questions regularly, zero startup cited as solving this.

**Net read:** No one aggregates buyers + verifies + executes sale + subdivides + delivers 3 individual khatas as a single productised flow. The whitespace is **real** and **tighter than farmland's** because the ingredient services (khata bifurcation, legal verification) already exist as unbundled commodities — TruePlots' contribution is orchestration, not novel operation.

**Caveat:** regulatory friction is the real risk, not competitive friction. BBMP bifurcation requires approved subdivision plan + minimum plot size (typically 600 sqft under BBMP rules) + legal partition deed + 2–6 month processing. Product timing hinges on whether this can be compressed.

## E. BBMP regulatory (brief)

- **Akrama-Sakrama:** Stayed by Supreme Court July 2025; no new applications accepted. Constitutional challenge pending. ([NoBroker](https://www.nobroker.in/blog/akrama-sakrama-scheme/))
- **B-Khata regularisation (separate track):** Karnataka Cabinet Aug 2025 approved regularisation of B-Khata properties issued by BBMP up to Sept 2024. Small sites exempted from CC/OC. ([LegalKart](https://www.legalkart.com/legal-blog/karnataka-cabinet-approves-b-khata-regularisation-what-bengaluru-property-owners-must-know), [Deccan Herald](https://www.deccanherald.com/india/karnataka/akrama-sakrama-20-karnataka-assembly-passes-bill-regularising-illegal-buildings-3687575))
- **B-Khata buyer constraints:** No home loan from mainstream banks; construction approvals restricted; resale discount 10–30% vs A-Khata typical. B→A conversion via regularisation fee now possible for eligible properties.
- **RERA on plots:** **Karnataka RERA requires registration for plotted developments >500 sqm or >8 units.** Applies explicitly to plotted developments. Mandates layout plan, approvals, promoter disclosure. 2/3 allottee consent needed for plan changes. ([KRERA](https://rera.karnataka.gov.in/aboutUs), [Pride Group](https://www.pridegroup.net/blog/everything-you-need-to-know-about-rera-approved-plots-in-karnataka))

**Critical implication for TruePlots:** If TruePlots positions as **facilitator** (not developer/promoter), RERA scope is lighter — only agent registration needed. If TruePlots takes SPV/promoter role in any subdivision, **RERA project registration triggers**. This is a legal opinion required before launch — the facilitator-vs-promoter line is the single most important structural question for urban sites.

## F. Fast-follow threat ranking

**Ranked most → least threatening to TruePlots urban:**

**1. NoBroker — HIGH threat, 6–12 month horizon**
- Has: 6,768 Bangalore plot inventory, legal verification as paid upsell, owner-direct positioning, funded scale, fast product velocity
- Missing from their stack: fractional / buyer-matching
- Could bolt on "verified + co-buy" in **one quarter** if TruePlots validates the demand
- Likely to ship BOTH (a) deeper verification and (b) fractional within 12 months post-TruePlots-traction

**2. 99acres — MEDIUM threat on verification, LOW on fractional, 12 month horizon**
- Largest plot inventory; "verified" badge is already marketing gimmick — upgrading to legal-grade is roadmap item
- BUT fractional is philosophically off-brand for InfoEdge's lead-gen DNA
- Likely: verification upgrade only within 12 months

**3. NBR Group / large BMRDA layout developers — MEDIUM threat on fractional on own stock, 6–9 month horizon**
- Already own plots, BBMP/BDA relationships, khata bifurcation playbook
- Lack marketplace/tech chops
- Could easily fractional their own unsold 60×40 as pre-subdivided 2×30×40 SKUs (developer-led pool model, not marketplace-led)
- Likely: fractional on captive stock within 12 months if demand validates

Lower threat: MagicBricks (lead-gen moat, low operational appetite), Homes247 (small scale), Square Yards (aggregator), Prestige/Sobha/Brigade (unwilling to fragment premium brand on fractional).

**Defensive posture read:** The moat in urban sites cannot be the marketplace UI or the verification report — both are replicable. **The moat must be operational muscle on BBMP khata bifurcation + buyer matching + escrow-based transaction architecture.** A marketplace-only TruePlots gets copied by NoBroker in one quarter.
