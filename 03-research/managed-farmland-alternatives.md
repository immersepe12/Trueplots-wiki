## What to look for in a managed-farmland alternative

Four criteria most buyers undervalue until they've been through one failed deal:

1. **Legal verification depth** — is land classification, encumbrance, PTCL status, and 79A/B applicability checked *before* you see the plot, or do you find out 8 weeks and ₹50K of lawyer fees later?
2. **Geographic breadth** — can you filter by a specific taluk, water source, or road access, or are you shown whatever the developer owns?
3. **Service completeness** — is registration, conversion, and construction included or available, or do you get a plot and good luck?
4. **Honest pricing transparency** — are maintenance fees, exit terms, and amenity escalations published, or only surfaced after sales call #3?

### 2. Swasya (Sannidhi, Sakleshpur; Vana Vida, Kanakapura)

Strong brand, single-developer inventory with 16,000+ trees, 4 lakes, full managed organic plantation. Claims up to 11% rental income via BnB operations.

- **Pricing:** ~₹309/sqft (Sannidhi) — 5–8× raw plantation land in same Hassan belt
- **Key weakness:** Sakleshpur is 4 hours from Bangalore — weakest location-to-use-case fit in the set for weekend retreats
- **Best for:** HNI buyers prioritising turnkey eco-retreat with BnB yield
- **Not ideal for:** Price-sensitive buyers, buyers needing <2hr drive

### 3. Sanctity Ferme (Shoolagiri + Coimbatore Aspira)

Strong track record — SF1 through SF3 sold out; SF4 and SF5 active; Aspira new launch at Coimbatore. Historical price curve ₹55/sqft → ₹450+/sqft.

- **Pricing:** ₹375/sqft starting (SF4); ₹50–65L entry plot
- **Location advantage:** 90 min from Bangalore to Shoolagiri — best location-to-price ratio in premium tier
- **Best for:** Chennai + Bangalore buyers wanting weekend retreat within 90 min + BnB income
- **Not ideal for:** Buyers expecting continued 8× appreciation from current price (curve already priced in)

### 4. Triguna Country Homes (Denkanikottai)

100-acre gated community with 7-acre lake; bundles Clarks Exotica resort life membership and 1,500 sqft Miyawaki forest per plot.

- **Pricing:** ₹399/sqft; ₹30L entry for 10,000 sqft plot
- **Distinctive feature:** Clarks Exotica membership is a soft-equity lock-in — valuable to some buyers, irrelevant to others
- **Best for:** Buyers who will actually use the Clarks Exotica facility
- **Not ideal for:** Buyers who see the resort membership as a gimmick

### 5. Hasiru Farms (multiple Karnataka locations)

129+ acres across 8+ projects in Sakleshpur, Kanakapura, Ramanagara. Parivar sub-brand serves the ₹21.5L entry-tier; main Hasiru brand is premium.

- **Pricing:** ₹21.5L entry (Parivar); ₹50–80L/acre premium tier (own benchmark)
- **Notable:** Hasiru publishes educational content ("10 Costly Errors", "Maintenance SLA Hidden Exclusions") that replicates much of the industry-transparency pitch
- **Best for:** Buyers who want managed farmland without Swasya-tier premium; appreciate dual-brand tier
- **Not ideal for:** Buyers wanting a single consistent brand experience

### 6. Mytan Farms (Bagepalli / Lepakshi)

Cheapest per-sqft in the set at ₹209, but weakest location — 2+ hours from Bangalore toward the Andhra border.

- **Pricing:** ₹209/sqft (Anandavana soft launch)
- **Key weakness:** Andhra-border location has lower raw land prices, meaning Mytan's premium multiple over local raw land may be the highest in the set despite the lowest sticker price
- **Best for:** Budget-tier buyers willing to trade drive time for sticker price
- **Not ideal for:** Buyers prioritising resale liquidity (thinner market)
