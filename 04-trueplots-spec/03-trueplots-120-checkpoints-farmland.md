# TruePlots Farmland — 120-Point Verification Checklist

*Karnataka-specific. Structured for SOP implementation by the verification ops team. Grouped into 10 public-facing categories (matches Spinny's "6 categories" pattern — publish categories, keep detailed points internal).*

---

## Public framing

"Every TruePlots farmland listing clears a 120-point legal and physical verification before publication. Here are the ten categories we check. The full 120-point report is included with every listing, downloadable after you register interest."

**Tier structure (mirrors Spinny Assured / Assured+ / Budget):**
- **TruePlots Verified Premium (120/120 clean)** — zero risk flags; highest-trust listings
- **TruePlots Verified (120 checked, ≤3 minor flags)** — transparently disclosed minor issues (e.g., pending mutation, small kharab ratio)
- **TruePlots Verified Conditional (flags present but resolvable)** — explicitly flagged with resolution pathway priced in

---

## Category 1 — Ownership & Title Chain (15 points)

*Confirms current seller has clean legal title derived from an unbroken chain.*

1. Current RTC (Record of Rights / Pahani) in seller's name
2. RTC matches sale deed being executed
3. Mother deed traced (original title document)
4. Chain of title reconstructed from mother deed to current seller
5. Every intermediate sale deed registered (verified via Kaveri SRO search)
6. Gift deeds in chain (if any) registered and stamp-duty-paid
7. Partition deeds in chain (if any) legally valid and registered
8. Will(s) in chain (if any) probated or reflected in mutation
9. Heir succession confirmed for any inheritance-based transfer
10. Legal heir certificate on file where relevant
11. No outstanding minor-grantor issue (minors as sellers require court approval)
12. Power-of-attorney (if seller is not principal) is registered and unrevoked
13. Seller's Aadhaar-PAN match ownership record
14. Seller is sole owner OR all co-owners identified and signing
15. Family tree verified if jointly-owned

## Category 2 — Revenue Records & Mutation (15 points)

*Bhoomi, Pahani, and land-records consistency checks.*

16. Bhoomi (Karnataka revenue portal) RTC extract current
17. Pahani shows no conflicting ownership claim
18. Mutation history (MR) reviewed from mother deed onward
19. Every sale in chain has corresponding mutation entry
20. Mutation dates plausibly follow registration dates
21. Survey number matches across RTC / sale deed / mother deed
22. Sub-division (hissa) numbers consistent across records
23. Extent (acres-guntas) consistent across all records
24. Boundary description consistent across records
25. Land classification (agricultural/converted) consistent across records
26. Revenue village, hobli, taluk, district all match
27. Index of Lands (ROR-II) reviewed
28. Tippani (land map) obtained and cross-checked
29. Atlas / akarbandh records consistent
30. No pending mutation flagged on Bhoomi portal

## Category 3 — Zoning, Land Use & Restrictions (15 points)

*The biggest source of late-stage deal failure.*

31. Land classification: Agricultural / Converted / Layout-approved
32. 79A status — was sold after July 2020 Amendment repeal OR seller is agriculturist
33. 79B status — same as above
34. PTCL check — not originally SC/ST grant land without permission transfer
35. PTCL Tahsildar query run for survey number
36. Tank bed (nala) land exclusion verified
37. Forest land status — not reserved, protected, or revenue forest
38. Forest Conservation Act 1980 not applicable
39. Greenbelt zone status under CDP (Comprehensive Development Plan)
40. CDP zone designation (agricultural / industrial / mixed / buffer)
41. Proximity to SEZs, industrial corridors, or acquisition notifications
42. Highway/NHAI acquisition notification check (widening plans)
43. Metro / rail corridor acquisition check
44. Airport Authority notification check
45. Reservoir / dam-backwater buffer zone check

## Category 4 — Boundary, Survey & Physical Extent (12 points)

*Ensures what you see matches what's on paper.*

46. KGIS (Karnataka Geographic Information System) cross-check against claimed boundary
47. Survey department official sketch obtained
48. 11E sketch if subdivision exists
49. Physical boundary markers present on-ground
50. Fencing / compound wall status
51. Kharab (waste) area percentage explicit
52. Bagayat (garden) vs Khushki (dry) vs Garudi (irrigated) classification
53. Slope and topography recorded
54. Photograph survey of all 4 boundaries
55. Neighbor land survey numbers confirmed via Bhoomi
56. No encroachment by/from adjacent parcels
57. Tree/crop inventory on parcel

## Category 5 — Encumbrance & Liens (8 points)

58. Encumbrance Certificate (EC) 30-year search from Kaveri
59. CERSAI search for registered mortgages
60. No pending bank loan on property
61. No court-ordered attachment
62. No income-tax department attachment
63. No cooperative bank / agricultural loan lien
64. No unpaid SRO dues or stamp-duty arrears on any prior sale
65. No notified irrigation-cess / water-charge arrears

## Category 6 — Road Access & Approach (10 points)

66. Road type — NH / SH / District / Panchayat / Revenue / Private
67. Road width verified via panchayat record
68. Frontage onto road in meters
69. Right-of-way registered if through private land
70. Easement rights documented for non-front-facing parcels
71. Distance from nearest paved road
72. Distance from nearest bus stop / railway station
73. Distance from district HQ
74. Google Maps route validates claimed access
75. Seasonal access (monsoon accessibility) verified

## Category 7 — Water Source & Irrigation (10 points)

76. Borewell present — yield in GPH and depth recorded
77. Open well present — yield recorded
78. Nearest water body (tank / lake / river) distance
79. Irrigation canal access status
80. Seasonal stream / hulla on/adjacent parcel
81. Rainfall zone classification (taluk-level)
82. Groundwater depth per regional aquifer map
83. Borewell permission status (where regulated)
84. Water allotment under any cooperative / society scheme
85. Pollution proximity (industrial effluent risk) check

## Category 8 — Litigation & Disputes (8 points)

86. Pending court cases on property (search via eCourts + Kaveri)
87. Revenue department pending notices
88. Family disputes affecting ownership
89. Tenancy claims under Karnataka Land Reforms Act
90. Historical tenant protection (Section 48A filings)
91. Partition suit history (family litigation)
92. Injunction / stay orders on parcel
93. Neighbor boundary disputes flagged

## Category 9 — Physical Site Verification (12 points)

*Done on-ground by TruePlots surveyor or field team.*

94. Site visit conducted with georeferenced photographs
95. All four boundaries walked and GPS-tagged
96. Boundary concurrence from at least 2 neighbors
97. Electricity pole / connection status on parcel
98. Any existing structure (farmhouse / shed) legality verified
99. Crops / plantation age and health
100. Soil type classification (observational)
101. Slope / levelling needs noted
102. Natural features (streams, rocks, hillocks) documented
103. Encroachment from grazing / squatter check
104. Access-road condition in monsoon (inference from neighbors)
105. Drone aerial photo (optional; Premium tier)

## Category 10 — Tax, Compliance & Developer/Layout-specific (15 points)

*Includes overlap layer if parcel is inside an approved layout.*

106. Property tax paid up-to-date at Gram Panchayat / Zilla Panchayat
107. No dues on electricity connection
108. Agricultural income records consistent (if claimed)
109. Seller has filed returns consistent with claimed agricultural income
110. Conversion order present if non-agricultural use claimed
111. Layout approval from BMRDA / DTCP / District Planning Authority (if layout)
112. Layout release certificate
113. Approved layout plan matches physical layout
114. Common infrastructure (internal road, drainage) delivered per plan
115. Developer escrow compliance (if project-sold plots)
116. Fragmentation Act compliance — parcel meets minimum extent for taluk
117. Tahsildar exemption certificate (if sub-minimum extent)
118. Panchayat NOC for farmhouse construction (if buyer intends)
119. Khata / patta status at Panchayat
120. Seller PAN-validated GST/TDS compliance on sale

## Ops implementation notes

### Automation breakdown

- **~45 points automatable** via Bhoomi API, CERSAI, KGIS, Kaveri, eCourts scrape: all of Category 2, most of Category 5, parts of Categories 1, 3
- **~40 points require document review** (sale deeds, mother deed, EC, mutation extracts): Categories 1, 2 (deep), 4, 8
- **~25 points require physical site visit**: Categories 4 (partial), 9, 10 (partial)
- **~10 points require Tahsildar / Panchayat office visit**: Categories 3 (PTCL query), 10 (layout-specific)

### Estimated cost per verification

- Premium (all 120): ₹8,000–12,000 internal cost
- Standard (90 core points): ₹4,000–6,000 internal cost

### Report output format

Same as Spinny's embedded-not-downloaded pattern on the listing page, PLUS a post-registration full PDF for the committed buyer.

On the listing page:
- Green/Yellow/Red dot per category (10 categories)
- Summary: "120 points checked. 115 clear, 3 minor flags, 2 resolved." (or whatever the result)
- "Download full verification report" CTA (gated behind email capture + registered interest)
